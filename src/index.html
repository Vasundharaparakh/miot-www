Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

How does Miotomo work with different books?
Miotomo has been trained on thousands of popular children's books and can generate meaningful conversations about almost any age-appropriate book. For specific titles, Miotomo asks specific questions designed by educators.
Is Miotomo safe for children?
Absolutely! Miotomo was designed specifically with safety as a top priority. All conversations are age-appropriate, and parents have access to a dashboard where they can review conversation history from children.
What type is Miotomo best for?
Miotomo is designed for children ages 6-12, with content that adapts to different reading and comprehension levels across various subjects and age groups.
How much does Miotomo cost?
Miotomo is currently in beta and free for early access users. After launch, pricing will be competitive and still have both free and premium subscription options for families.
Can parents see what their children are discussing?
Yes, parents have access to a dashboard where they can review conversation history, track reading progress, and see skill development over time.
